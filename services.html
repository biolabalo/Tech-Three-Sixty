Instanta – Fleet is a web-based fleet management software used by fleet managers to: Quickly report vehicle maintenance history, fueling history and their associated cost. Better track vehicle documents expiration through automated notifications Schedule trips, assign drivers and dispatch vehicles. Create and send trip invoice to client and receive payments online. Generate instant vehicle profitability report. Coordinate vehicle lease activities, lease invoices and payments.

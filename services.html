Instanta – Fleet is a web-based fleet management software used by fleet managers to:

  • Quickly report vehicle maintenance history,
  • fueling history and their associated cost.
  • Better track vehicle documents expiration through automated notifications Schedule trips, assign drivers and dispatch vehicles.
  • Create and send trip invoice to client and receive payments online.
  • Generate instant vehicle profitability report.
  • Coordinate vehicle lease activities, lease invoices and payments.

Instanta Facility Manager is an easy to use web based CMMS (computerized maintenance management system) for facility managers and property owners who want to :

  • Provide a simple online interface for students and/or supervisors to submit maintenance request.
  • Easily create, assign, cost, and monitor work orders.
  • Objectively appraise vendors & technicians performance using criteria such as SLA compliance, job satisfaction rating and frequency of repeated jobs.
  • Track facilities and assets maintenance history and cost.
  • Receive automated reminders for planned preventive and predictive maintenance.
  • Perform stock inventory, track material usage and reorder levels.
  • Receive and track end user feedbacks and job satisfaction ratings.
  • Easily collaborate with all stakeholders via SMS and Email.
